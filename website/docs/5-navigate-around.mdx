### Using the bubbles

Each bubble on the stage can be dragged around by dragging the `header` of the bubble. Also you can resize each bubble on the stage by dragging the edges.

By clicking the `collapse` button on the header, you can collapse a bubble.

### Panning the stage

If you click an drag on an empty area on the stage, you will be able to pan the stage.

### Overview of the stage

If you want to take a quick overview of the stage, press `.` key and it will open an overview window. Clicking on any item on this window will take you to the bubble on stage.
